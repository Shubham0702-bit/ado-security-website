Our Story

Since 1985, ADO Security & Protection Services has been a leader in providing trusted, professional security solutions across Mumbai and beyond. Our commitment to excellence and reliability has earned the confidence of residential, commercial, and event clients alike.

1985

Founded as a local security agency

We began as a small but dedicated team serving residential communities in Mumbai.

1995

Expanded to industrial & event protection

Our services grew to cover industrial premises and large-scale event security needs.

2005

Launched city-wide night patrolling

We introduced mobile patrolling teams for comprehensive community safety around the clock.

2025

100+ clients & 500+ security professionals

Today, we serve a diverse client base with a highly trained, disciplined security force.
