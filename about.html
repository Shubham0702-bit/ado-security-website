About ADO Security & Protection Services

Established in 1985 in Mumbai, ADO Security & Protection Services has grown to become one of the region’s most trusted and professional security agencies.

With decades of experience, we proudly provide end-to-end security solutions across residential complexes, industrial premises, factories, showrooms, and corporate spaces — not just in Mumbai, but also across Gujarat and other states.

At ADO, we believe that security is not just about presence — it's about preparedness. Every member of our security staff is rigorously trained, verified, and disciplined to handle real-world challenges with professionalism and confidence.

Our commitment to trust, reliability, and quick response has earned us the confidence of clients from all sectors — residential societies, industrial hubs, and even event organizers.

Our mission is to deliver peace of mind through our professional team of watchmen,bouncers, and patrolling officers.
